    Order and Delivery Times


    Unfortunately we only hold a limited amount of stock, and many items in our online shop have to be ordered from our suppliers. In the online shop, we indicate where items are not currently in-stock, and where delivery will be delayed.

    Casualwear (printed t-shirts, hoodies, etc.):

    For items which are not currently in-stock, we order from our supplier on the first working day of the month, with delivery approximately three to four weeks later. For example, an item ordered on the 20th of September, you should expect delivery at the end of October. For an item ordered on the 4th of October, you should expect delivery at the end of November.

    Technical Kit (jerseys, gilets, running tops, etc.):

    We generally order technical kit four times a year (spring, summer, autumn and winter) from our supplier. Our next order will be placed on the 7th of August 2017, with delivery expected in early September. Orders received after the 7th of August will be ordered in early November for delivery in mid-December.

    Questions?

    If you have any questions about the ordering process or delivery timescales, please contact us.
