Welcome to the Boldmere Bullets

The Boldmere Bullets are a cycling and running collective from Sutton Coldfield. Formed in autumn 2013, the Bullets has become one of the West Midlands' largest free sporting communities, bringing together hundreds of people to make new friends, and share each others' interest in our sports.

The spirit of the collective is friendship and a love of cycling, walking, running and socialising. You pay nothing to join us, just bring your enthusiasm and commitment. Our values are 'free', 'fun', and 'flexible' - we want everyone who joins one of our activities to have a great time with us. Learn about the history of the Boldmere Bullets.

Boldmere Bullets and Charity

The Boldmere Bullets are committed to supporting our local community in Sutton Coldfield and helping out wherever we can. We have an annual charity of the year, which in 2017 is The Lisieux Trust - a fantastic group supporting disabled people in the community. All our our major events this year will be raising money to support this worthy cause.

In 2016 we raised £33,400 for Breast Friends, and in 2015 we raised over £10,000 for the Sutton Coldfield Cancer Support Centre!
