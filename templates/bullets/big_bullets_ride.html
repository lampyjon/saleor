    10/10 For Acacia Mums

    What is 10/10 For Acacia Mums? Put simply, it is the Boldmere Bullets' big collective effort for 2018 to raise money for our Charity of the Year, Acacia Family Support. Acacia are a great charity providing support to mums and families affected by ante or postnatal depression (PND) in Birmingham. The event on Sunday 16 September will provide runs, rides, and other exciting activities, to help to raise money for this great group.

    Our goal for the day is to run and ride over 10,000km - all tracked online! We'll all start at Boldmere Gate in Sutton Park, departing

    The rides and runs are free to participate in, although we would encourage you to make a donation to Acacia via Just Giving, or to consider raising sponsorship.
