Bullets Ride Routes


The Boldmere Bullets Cycling Collective post the ride routes out on Strava in advance of the ride. Upcoming routes can be found at https://www.strava.com/clubs/BoldmereBulletsCyclingCollective. If you would like to try some of the previous routes used by the Bullets, here are some good ones. With the exception of a few of the shorter rides, they all start and end at either in Boldmere or at The Royal Hotel in Sutton Coldfield. Click on the distance to open details of the route on Strava.
